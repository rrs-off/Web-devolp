Pasta Carbonara Chocolate Cake

Pasta Carbonara

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Eggs 4 large
Parmesan Cheese 100g
Pancetta 150g
Garlic 2 cloves
Black Pepper To taste

Steps:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a bowl, whisk eggs with grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Fry pancetta and garlic in a pan until crispy.
  4. Drain spaghetti and mix with pancetta (remove garlic if preferred).
  5. Remove pan from heat, stir in the egg mixture, and mix well.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 200g
Sugar 200g
Cocoa Powder 50g
Butter 200g
Eggs 4 large
Baking Powder 1 tsp

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a cake tin.
  2. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Sift in flour, cocoa powder, and baking powder. Mix until combined.
  5. Pour batter into the cake tin and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool before serving.
